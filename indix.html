  • Scientific Name: Cygnus

  • Average Lifespan: 15-20 years

The white swan is commonly called the “Mute Swan” (Cygnus olor) and is one of the most widespread birds in the United Kingdom. It is distinguished by its long, curved neck, white body,and orange-red bill with a black base The American white swan uses a variety of visual signals to convey aggression, calm, and alert behaviors It feeds in shallow waters on fish, crustaceans, and amphibians.
